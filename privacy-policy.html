Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data protection are important to us.

1. Introduction

HassleFreeDrive provides professional driver services and values user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide to us directly and information that is automatically collected when you use our services:

  • Personal Identity: Name, Phone number, and Email address.
  • Location Data: Real-time location to facilitate booking and tracking.
  • Driver Information: Driver license information and background verification documents (for drivers).
  • Activity Details: Trip history, booking details, and payment preferences.

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected data to provide and improve our services:

  • To connect customers with professional drivers efficiently.
  • To verify driver identity and maintain a secure community.
  • To manage ride services and handle booking requests.
  • To improve service quality through feedback and analytics.
  • To provide timely customer support and assistance.

4. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to ensure your data is stored securely. We do not share your personal information with unauthorized third parties. Access to your data is strictly limited to authorized personnel involved in service delivery.

5. Location Usage

Your location information is used to assign the nearest available driver, provide accurate ETAs, and improve overall service efficiency. Users have control over location permissions through their device settings.

6. Third-Party Sharing

We may share necessary information with:

  • Payment Providers: To process transactions securely.
  • Verification Agencies: For background checks and safety compliance.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

7. User Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal data. You can manage your information through the app settings or by contacting our support team.

8. Policy Updates

HassleFreeDrive reserves the right to update this policy periodically. We will notify users of any significant changes via the app or email.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy practices, please contact us at:

support@hasslefreedrive.com
